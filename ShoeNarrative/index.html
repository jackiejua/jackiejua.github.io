Sneaker Seeker

A dive into Jackie's top owned and wanted shoes of 2023

The Reason

I'm a slowly-developing sneakerhead. After over a year of working as a shoe sale associate, it naturally became a topic of interest. I started learning more about brands, styles, and everything that makes the "sneaker game" so popular and expensive.

I wanted to take this opportunity to create my own online wardrobe as a way to rank what I have and what I want. It's a collection of my past shoes as well as my future ones.

The goal is to help me get a better understanding of what shoes I prefer and where the commonalities are. It could also reduce impulse shoe buying because I'll already have shoes that I know I want, and I can save up for them instead of buying something I'll rarely wear.

The Breakdown

After gathering data by searching the Champs Sports, Footlocker, Vans, and Converse websites, as well as retrieving my past receipts, I used the information found there, along with my own thoughts and feelings, to break every down shoe into eight categories:

  • Name
  • Brand
  • Colorway
  • Comfortability (1-10)
  • Price
  • Type
  • Appearance (1-10)
  • Status (Owned or Wanted)
